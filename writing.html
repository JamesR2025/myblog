Emptiness and Fullness

A farmer builds a dam
To be filled with water,
In preperation for the harvest.

A warrior sharpens his sword,
His body and mind,
In preparation for battle.

A tiger crouches, and stalks
It's prey
In preperation for the killing bite.

A difference in charges builds
Released in an instant.

Lightning tears through the sky.
Setting the oak ablaze

Energy accumulates in reserves
To be released at the proper time
In accordance with the Way

Consistency
